Why Vocab Master?

The Problem

  • Busy adults have limited time to devote to expanding their vocabulary.
  • Adults need a way to categorize, reference, and study new vocabulary and concepts so they can advance in their chosen field.

The Objective

  • Provide adults with a tool that they can use to learn vocabulary on-the-go.
  • Provide adults with a customizable tool so they can learn vocabulary and concepts that will help them advance their career.

Competitive Analysis - Let's see what others are doing.

Pros

Flash Cards Plus has a very simple navigation making it easy to create cards and add decks. There is an option for talk-to-text. They use familiar icons that users would expect to see. Users can also shuffle cards and remove already mastered cards from the deck. Users do not need to create a profile, removing a step.

Cons

There are numerous ad interruptions that make task completion difficult and make it frustrating to use.

Pros

Flash Cards.io is simple and easy to use with familiar icons. It has a night shift mode which makes it easier to use in bed or as a vehicle passenger at night. There is a share feature which helps connect users to others. No ads make for minimal distraction. There is the option to upload images to cards to make studying more visually stimulating.

Cons

If the user does not focus on the onboarding process, the use of the app is confusing at times and is not as intuitive as it should be. Onboarding also neglects to display all of the available options which creates confusion when using the app.

Pros

Study Blue allows users to identify a connection to their school and specific classes. It Allows users to connect to others by uploading decks that others can access. No ads make for distraction-free environment. The flows are straightforward and easy to navigate. Familiar icons are used.

User Research: Let's learn about our word enthusiasts.

Research Goals

  • Identify what makes learning new words enjoyable.
  • Identify what makes learning vocabulary challenging.
  • Understand users' study sessions - where, with whom, and how long study sessions are.
  • Identify methods/stategies used in the past when studying words.

In-person User Interviews (4 Participants)

4 adults were interviewed (via in-person or Skype) about their vocabulary learning experiences.

View the full interview data here.

"I really enjoy using new words in context because I'm always looking for new ways to express myself."

- Teacher"

"I usually study for short periods of time by myself but then I try to use the terms with colleagues and friends."

- Neuroscientist

"I like to use flash cards, memorable sentences, small illustrations or cartoons, and mnemonic devices."

- Children's Librarian

Solutions

  1. Allow users to create an account so they can save their flash cards.
  2. Allow users to create custom flash cards to suit their personal needs.
  3. Allow users to organize their flash cards into folders.
  4. Allow users to quickly and easily access and practice their flash cards.
