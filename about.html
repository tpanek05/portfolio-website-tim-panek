About Me

My Story

I am a professional counselor turned UX designer based in Randolph, NJ looking for opportunities that will challenge my skills and help me to grow professionally. As a counselor, I gained 15 years of experience in understanding clients’ unique life experiences so that I could craft individualized plans that helped improve their quality of life. As such, I am passionate about user-centered design and believe that by truly understanding the user, I can develop creative solutions which will make his/her life easier. I am available for full-time and freelance work opportunities.

When I’m not working on my designs, I’m working on my bowling game. I’ve been bowling for 26 years. I have bowled 13 perfect games and my collegiate bowling team was inducted into the Lindenwood University Athletic Hall of Fame in 2018 for having won the university’s national bowling title in 2005. Bowling is my time to reduce stress, recharge my battery, and have a good time.
